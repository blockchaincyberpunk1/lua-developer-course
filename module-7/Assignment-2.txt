Lua Community Project Exploration

Instructions:
For this assignment, you will explore and evaluate open-source Lua projects within the Lua community.

Research and identify three open-source Lua projects that align with your interests or career goals. These projects can be related to game development, web development, scientific computing, or any other field.

Examine the project repositories, README files, and documentation to understand the project's purpose, scope, and contributors.

Select one of the identified projects and perform one or more of the following tasks:

Clone the project repository and build it on your local machine.
Explore the codebase to understand its structure and components.
Submit a documentation improvement or a code comment enhancement to the project.
Document your findings, including project descriptions, your chosen project's details, and the contribution you made or attempted.

Submit your documentation of the projects you explored, including project descriptions and evidence of your contribution or exploration.
